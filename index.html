What is a dream job?

A dream job is a position that combines an activity, skill or passion with a
moneymaking opportunity. They can be exciting and glamorous, like acting or playing music, or
they can be prestigious and high-paying like working as a
doctor or lawyer. Dream jobs differ from person to person, but they usually
share a few common attributes:

  • They are engaging: Dream jobs keep you engaged and challenged.
  • They help others:Dream jobs help others directly or indirectly
  • They use your talents: Dream jobs use your specific talents and skills.
  • They include excellent colleagues: Dream jobs have supportive, capable
  • They maintain a positive workspace: Dream jobs have an upbeat and transparent company culture.
